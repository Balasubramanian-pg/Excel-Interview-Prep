### 29. **How do you calculate compound interest?**

Formula: =P*(1+r/n)^(n*t)

- P = Principal (initial amount)
- r = Annual interest rate (decimal)
- n = Compounding periods per year
- t = Years

Excel: =A1*(1+B1/C1)^(C1*D1)
Or using FV: =FV(rate, nper, pmt, [pv], [type])
