### 101. **How do you calculate compound growth rate (CAGR)?**

=(EndValue/StartValue)^(1/Years)-1

Excel formula:
=(B10/B1)^(1/9)-1

Or using RRI function:
=RRI(Years, -StartValue, EndValue)
