### 129. **How do you calculate working capital metrics?**

**Current Ratio:**
=Current_Assets / Current_Liabilities

**Quick Ratio (Acid Test):**
=(Current_Assets - Inventory) / Current_Liabilities

**Working Capital:**
=Current_Assets - Current_Liabilities

**Working Capital Ratio:**
=Working_Capital / Total_Assets
