### 89. **What is XMATCH?**

Modern replacement for MATCH:
Syntax: =XMATCH(lookup_value, lookup_array, [match_mode], [search_mode])

Same modes as XLOOKUP

Example with INDEX:
=INDEX(DataRange, XMATCH(Value, LookupRange, 0))
